Evergreen is a single family home neighborhood on the glimmering shores of Lake Tamarack. The development was built in the late 1980s by Kennedy Homes. With 191 homes, there are 6 different floors plans, ranging in size from approximately 2,200 square feet to approximately 3,400 square feet.

Location

Evergreen is located in Hoffman Estates, Illinois on Thornbark, North of Palatine Rd and west of Ela. The subdivision is located within the Elementary School District 15 and High School District 211. It is conveniently located within minutes to shopping, 2 train stations, several forest preserves and access to the I-90 Interstate.

For Residents

Residents of Evergreen are encouraged to register their household with the HOA Communication Committee. All information provided will only be used by the Evergreen HOA Board/Committees for communication and will not be shared with third parties.

Evergreen HOA is also on several social media platforms. These social media communities are private and managed by the HOA Communication Committee. Upcoming events, board meetings and meeting notes are posted on these social media communities.
